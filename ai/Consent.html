You are being asked to participate in a research study. The purpose of the study is to examine how individuals acting as supply chain managers, view the forecast results provided by Artificial Intelligence. Taking part in this study is voluntary, and choosing not to take part will not affect your standing in any employment, class or other. The investigators will explain the study to you, and will answer any questions you might have.

Your participation in this research study involves indicating whether or not you accept the predictions provided by an Artificial Intelligence method. The questions will be presented in an online experiment. This is a one-time activity and there will be no direct interaction with the investigators or with other participants. The experiment involves questions such as “Week 34 forecast value: 377 units. Will you keep it?” and will take you approximately 30 minutes.

It is your choice whether or not you take part in this study. If you agree to participate and then choose to withdraw from the study, that is your right, and your decision will not be held against you.

This research study is not expected to pose any additional risks beyond what you would normally experience in your regular everyday life. However, the possible risks specific to this research study include similar to a person’s everyday use of the internet and that there is always a risk of breach of confidentiality. Participation in this study may involve loss of privacy and the potential for breach in confidentiality. As with any use of electronic means to store data, there is a breach of data privacy. UNT does not provide medical services, or financial assistance for emotional distress or injuries that might happen from participating in this research. If you do experience any discomfort, please inform the research team. If you need is urgent, helpful resources include the national Suicide and Crisis hotline at 988. Study data will be physically and electronically secured by the research team. As with any use of electronic means to store data, there is a risk of breach of data security.

Compensation for participation is not being provided by the University of North Texas researchers. Upon experiment completion you may be compensated in the amount previously agreed upon when you started the experiment by Prolific. Any compensation is directly provided by Prolific. If you choose not to complete part or all of study procedures, or if you fail attention checks, you may not be compensated for participation. If you have questions or would like to discuss issues regarding your compensation, please contact Prolific. You may also discuss this with the research team at paul.owusu@unt.edu

You will be compensated $3 in the form of a payment on the Prolific Platform immediately when your part in the study is complete.

The research team will store your personal information in a separate and secure location away from your study data and maintain your confidentiality in all publications/presentations to do with this research.

Access to research study will be limited to people who have a need to review this information. All paper and electronic data collected from this study will be stored in a secure location on the UNT campus and/or a secure UNT server for at least three (3) years past the end of this research in a password protected computer in PI’s campus office.

The results of this study may be published and/or presented without naming you as a participant. The data collected about you for this study will be used for future research studies that are not described in this consent form. If that occurs, an IRB would first evaluate the use of any information that is identifiable to you, and confidentiality protection would be maintained. While absolute confidentiality cannot be guaranteed, the research team will make every effort to protect the confidentiality of your records, as described here and to the extent permitted by law.

This research examines whether or not you accept the predictions provided by an Artificial Intelligence method. You will be asked a series of questions about this survey that should take 30 minutes to complete. After completing the survey, you will be paid $3 for your participation. We do not ask for your name or any other information that might identify you. You may withdraw at any time any you may choose not to answer any question but you must proceed to the final screen of the study in order to receive your completion code, which you must submit in order to be paid. In accordance with Prolific policies, we may reject your work if the HIT was not completed correctly or the instructions were not followed.
